About Shlobo Scaffolding

Proudly South African, serving KwaZulu-Natal with excellence

Our Story

Established in 2021, Shlobo Scaffolding operates under Shlobo Construction and Trading (Pty) Ltd, specializing in delivering scaffolding, engineering, and construction services of the highest quality. As a 100% black male-owned Level 1 B-BBEE Exempted Micro Enterprise, we are deeply committed to empowerment, safety, and service excellence.

Our Vision

To become KwaZulu-Natal's leading provider of innovative, safe, and client-focused scaffolding and construction solutions.

Our Mission

To deliver cost-effective, timely, and sustainable engineering and scaffolding services that exceed client expectations and support the development of South Africa's infrastructure.

We are accredited B-BBEE Level 1 contributor and we pride ourselves for producing quality service all the time.
